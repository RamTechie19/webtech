COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer science is the study of algorithms, computation, and information processing. It encompasses theoretical foundations, practical applications, and the impact of computing on society.

ENGINEERING

Engineering applies scientific and mathematical principles to design, develop, and maintain structures, machines, systems, and processes, aiming to solve practical problems.

LAW

Law is a system of rules created and enforced through social or governmental institutions to regulate behavior, with the aim of upholding justice, fairness, and order in society.

LIBERAL STUDIES

Liberal studies explore diverse fields such as humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and the arts. It encourages critical thinking, creativity, and interdisciplinary understanding.

DESIGN

Design involves creating solutions with a focus on aesthetics, functionality, and usability. It encompasses various disciplines such as graphic design, industrial design, and user experience design.

MANAGEMENT

Management studies focus on organizing, planning, directing, and controlling resources to achieve organizational goals. It covers areas such as leadership, strategy, operations, and human resources.

HEALTH SCIENCE

Health science encompasses various disciplines related to human health, including medicine, nursing, public health, nutrition, and allied health professions. It focuses on promoting and maintaining health and well-being.

MEDIA

Media studies examine the production, distribution, and consumption of various forms of media, including print, broadcast, digital, and social media. It explores the cultural, social, and political impact of media on society.
